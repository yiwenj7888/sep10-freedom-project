What is Business?

An organization engaged in commercial, industrial, or professional activities to provide goods or services in exchange for money or value.

  • Goal: Earn profit through innovation
  • Scales: Small shops to large corporations

Present: Technological Innovations

1884: Switches

Enhanced network performance ensuring uninterrupted flow for businesses.

1950s: Mainframe Computers

Used for complex calculations and record keeping.

1990s: Data Centers

Enabled large-scale data storage and analysis.

1999: Salesforce

Improved customer relationship and sales performance.

2006: Xero

Cloud-based accounting with real-time data access.

2020: Productivity Glasses

Helps employees organize tasks and increase efficiency through smart glasses technology.

2020: Emotional Data Analytics

Real-time analysis of customer emotion across digital platforms.

2020: Helm Schedule Optimizer

Optimizes cash flow and helps in confident decision-making.
